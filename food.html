Benny's Food

Benny gets 105 grams of food mixed with water, a little less than 1/4 teaspoon of Miralax, and a 75mg gabapentin at each meal and 5mg of fluoxetine at dinner otherwise he would be fat, constipated, and anxious. He eats Friskies paté. The fluoxetine goes in a chicken- or salmon-flavored Feline Greenies pill pocket. Sometimes the pill pocket falls out of his mouth two or three times before he swallows it because he only has three teeth. I usually give him breakfast and my partner usually gives him dinner. Here is a comparison of how I prepare Benny's food and how my partner prepares Benny's food:

How I Prepare Benny's Food

  1. Get a fork.
  2. Take the food out of fridge.
  3. Measure the food (open a new can if the old can doesn't have 105g in it.)
  4. Sprinkle the Miralax on the food.
  5. Add 3 tablespoons of hot water to the food.
  6. Mash the food with the fork until it's a disgusting soup.
  7. Sprinkle the gabapentin capsule over the food.
  8. Serve Benny the food.

How My Partner Prepares Benny's Food

  1. Get a spoon.
  2. Take the food out of fridge.
  3. Measure the food (open a new can if the old can doesn't have 105g in it.)
  4. Smooth the food with the back of the spoon.
  5. Add enough cold water to make a moat around the food.
  6. Sprinkle the Miralax on the food.
  7. Sprinkle the gabapentin capsule over the food.
  8. Serve Benny the food.
