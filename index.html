Benny the Cat

Benny is our cat.

He was supposed to be trapped, neutered, and returned but he became very friendly while the TNR organization had him so they put him in a foster home until we adopted him last summer. He has feline immunodeficiency virus (FIV) and feline hyperesthesia syndrome (FHS). He weighed 19.35 pounds when we adopted him and his teeth were so rotten that all but three had to be removed.

He loves cuddling with us and biting us and aside from my partner, he's my best friend.
